About Us

Our Story

Since 2012, Mera Parivar Foundation has been at the forefront of family welfare in India. What started as a small initiative to support struggling families has grown into a movement that touches thousands of lives across 20 districts.

Our holistic approach addresses the needs of every family member ‚Äî from providing quality education to children, empowering women with vocational skills, to ensuring dignified care for senior citizens. We believe that when families are strong, communities flourish.

Our Programs

Comprehensive programs designed to address the diverse needs of families and create lasting positive change.

Family Welfare
